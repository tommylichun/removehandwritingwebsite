Privacy Policy - Paper Restore King

Last updated: 2026-03-20

1. Data We Collect

  • Content you provide or create in the app: scanned document images (for handwriting removal), question/answer images (for wrong questions), and any answer text you enter (including AI-generated answers).
  • Device/App information needed to provide features: platform (iOS), selected output quality (1K / 2K), language setting (used to decide the AI reply language), and your app version (passed as metadata in the handwriting removal request).
  • Ads and credits: if you watch rewarded ads to earn credits, AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect ad-related information according to Google’s policies. The app also stores today’s watched count locally to enforce a daily cap.
  • Purchases: in-app purchases are handled via StoreKit 2. Payment is processed by Apple; the app updates your local credit balance after successful verification.
  • Contact: if you email us, the content of your message (including your email address) is provided to us.

2. How We Use Data

  • Handwriting removal: the app compresses images and sends them (base64) to our Cloudflare Worker endpoint (/api/remove-handwriting). The Worker calls OpenRouter and its model providers to process the images and returns the processed results. The app then displays and lets you export the output.
  • AI Q&A / wrong question answers: the app sends the selected question image (base64) to (/api/ask-question) together with the selected model, a max-words setting, and the language preference. The Worker calls OpenRouter and returns a structured text answer, which is saved to your wrong-question entry on your device.
  • Wrong question management and exports: your documents and wrong questions are stored locally. PDF/JPG/PNG exports are generated on-device and delivered through iOS sharing.
  • Credits and daily limits: we manage costs and credit balance locally, and use the stored daily watch count to limit rewarded ads per day.

3. Sharing and Third Parties

  • Cloudflare Workers: used to route and process requests.
  • OpenRouter and its model providers: used for image processing (handwriting removal) and text generation (AI Q&A).
  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads: used to display rewarded ads and grant credits.
  • Apple / StoreKit 2: used for in-app purchase transactions.
  • Your selected sharing destination: exported files are shared using the iOS share flow.

4. Local Storage and Retention

  • Local storage: the app stores documents/wrong questions metadata and the images you provide or capture in the app’s local storage on your device.
  • Cloud processing: once a request is sent, Cloudflare Workers and third-party services process the data and may retain request-related logs or records according to their policies.
  • Deletion: you can delete documents or wrong questions inside the app to remove local content.

5. Your Choices

  • You can stop using handwriting removal and AI Q&A features.
  • You can choose whether to watch rewarded ads to earn credits.
  • You can manage (including deleting/exporting) your documents and wrong questions in the app.
  • If you have privacy questions, contact us by email.
